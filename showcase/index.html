Welcome to YURS 2016!


Present your Research. Explore Diverse Fields. Inspire New Ideas.


YURS 2016 will showcase 25 incredible undergraduate researchers and their projects, exploring cutting-edge topics in diverse fields of study. Through a two-hour poster session, presenters will engage with members of the community to share their projects and inspire new ideas.
All are welcome to attend!
