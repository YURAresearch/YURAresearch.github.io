YURC 2017

Workshops

Sunday, February 12th
2:00 - 3:15 PM

Commercializing Research Workshop

Grand Ballroom

Kyle Jensen, the Associate Dean and Shanna & Eric Bass Director of Entrepreneurship at the Yale School of Management, will provide you with the foundational information and insider advice to successfully profit from your research ideas in only one hour. This founder of Pitrho, PriorSmart and Agrivida and MIT alumnus is an expert at developing businesses from scientific innovations and marketing intellectual property and navigating the patenting process.



Fellowships Workshop

Church Breakout Room

Featuring Samantha Olyha, an M.D./PhD candidate at Yale School of Medicine and a Marshall Scholar, and Javier Portillo, a PhD student at Yale University and NSF Graduate Research Fellow, this workshop will discuss how to identify the fellowships you are eligible for and how to successfully prepare and apply for them, concentrating on competitive national and international opportunities for post-graduates.
